Policy

Exception Override Reasons

  1. Natural Disaster/Extreme Weather
  2. House/Business Fire
  3. Death in the Family
  4. Card Member's /family member's extended hospitalization
  5. Bank account frozen due to ID theft/fraud
  6. Discrepancy in Autopay Effective Date (date of first automatic payment misunderstood)

Things to check when asking TL for override for Late and Interest charge waiver request:

  • Check Member Since
  • Previous adjustments made in Balance Summary
  • Promote auto pay to avoid late fees and interest charges (whenever we process waiver)

Procedure

GSP > Transactions > Transaction Description

click on "Purchase Finance Charge"
