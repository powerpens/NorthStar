$200 AIRLINE FEE CREDIT‡

Select one qualifying airline and then receive up to $200 in statement credits per calendar year when incidental fees, such as checked bags and in-flight refreshments, are charged by the airline to your Platinum Card® Account.*‡

FEE CREDIT FOR GLOBAL ENTRY OR TSA PRE✓®‡

When you apply for and receive your Global Entry or TSA Pre✓®‡, you get:

• Expedited screening at select airports

• A credit for the application fees on your statement.

If approved for Global Entry, you will receive access to TSA Pre✓® at no additional charge. Receive one statement credit every 4 years after you apply for Global Entry ($100) or every 4.5 years after you apply for TSA Pre✓® ($85) with an eligible Platinum Card®. Each Additional Card on eligible Card accounts is also eligible for one statement credit for each application fee. To receive the statement credit, the application fee must be charged on the eligible Additional Card.

When you use your Eligible Card to reserve and pay for the Entire Rental and decline the collision damage waiver at the rental Company counter, you can be covered for Damage to or Theft of a Rental Vehicle in a Covered Territory. Please read important exclusions and restrictions. Not all vehicle types or rentals are covered. Coverage is not available for vehicles rented in Australia, Italy, and New Zealand. This product provides secondary coverage and does not include liability coverage.
