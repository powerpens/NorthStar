Easy.Recognize.Solve

Updated: 08 Jun 2020

Opening

  • Acknowledge - Always acknowledge the customer first before providing resolution
  • Empathy - Do not assume the emotion of it is not started. (Do NOT say " I understand you are frustrated"
  • Assurance - build trust and confidence

Middle

  • Probing Questions (to know the right resolution)
  • Positive Scripting - avoid "unfortunately" and negative phrases. Focus on what we can do
  • Provide Options/Alternate Solutions/Referral

Closing

  • Value
  • Self-service options
  • Power Recap
